## Builder Lessons

### 1. Vertical Data Moats Win

50% of analyzed startups are building industry-specific data advantages. Generic solutions without proprietary data are struggling to differentiate.

**Apply it:** Identify a narrow vertical. Build data collection into your core loop. Make your product better with every customer interaction.

### 2. Agentic Is the New Baseline

With 50% adoption among analyzed startups, autonomous AI architectures are moving from differentiator to expectation.

**Apply it:** Design for agent-first workflows. Build APIs that agents can call. Think about tool use, not just chat interfaces.

### 3. Trust Infrastructure Matters

Guardrail and safety layers appeared in 7 of 16 analyzed startups. Enterprise buyers are demanding AI governance.

**Apply it:** Build observability and audit trails from day one. Consider guardrails as a feature, not an afterthought.
